HELP IN RESUCE OPS

Interested in helping in the rescue operations, then click the below button to help the needy along with the Zulip bot:

    Steps to follow:

  1. Signup or login with an OAuth
  2. Click on a message to start chatting with the DistressSOS bot
  3. Enter '@DistressSOS report', to find locations requesting help
  4. Enter '@DistressSOS rescue <serial_number>', to remove the location corresponding to that serial number from the distress list
